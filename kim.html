Confidence Chats with Random Strangers

V: So my project is mainly about confidence and where it comes from in people’s day to day lives. Would you say you experience confidence in your day to day life?

K: Different levels based on the day, as far as where it comes from I think it could have a lot to do with what you do on a day to day basis and if that brings you a good feeling, to do what your job is or what you do after your job, just kinda like your impact you make

V: What is some piece of advice, or helpful tips you have to share with people who struggle with confidence?

K: Always better yourself, do something that can improve yourself, and it will always make you feel better in the end, completing some type of self goal.

V: Thank you so much for letting me take your picture! It’s kind of important to me to analyze, especially for the younger generation how social media doesn’t have to impact your life as much as one might think it does

K: And if social media can do something for confidence it should be positive feedback. Old school rule if you can’t say something nice don’t say anything
