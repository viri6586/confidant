Demi

Confidence Chats with Friends

V: Do you have any forms of social media?

V: Would you say you experience confidence in your day to day life?

D: I would say I’m pretty confident yeah

V: Ok where does that confidence come from?

D: I think from my mom because as a kid we were told we were like the most beautiful things in the world every single day by our parents so like regardless if I ever thought I was ugly my mom was like “nope, you’re gorgeous” so I was just like “oh I'm so pretty, all the time” and it just stuck with me

V: Do you think that you just wake up and think to yourself “I am beautiful, I am amazing” and that's what gives you confidence?

D: Ya I think it was just instilled in me because I just wake up knowing those things, I mean of course you have a bad day and you’ll be like “ugh I look like a$$ f*ck today” you know? But at the end you just have to be like “I’m beautiful.” I really mean that.

V: What would be some advice to share with someone who doesn’t feel confident?

D: I would definitely find something about yourself that you can really focus on so that you can have a bad day and feel ugly but then you can be like “I have really awesome hair” so you know your hair will always be good, just like as an example. I think that people rely too much on what other people think of them, and so that’s how come they don’t feel confident or they feel ugly because they think everyone else out there thinks that they're ugly or they don’t fit the standard of what everyone else thinks is ‘hot’ or whatever. So i think they need to stop doing things for other people, you should only do things for yourself and not care what anyone else thinks.

V: Ok so do you think that social media affects your confidence?

D: Yeah yes and no I don’t have a Facebook or things that like lets people in, so the pictures I do have, or post or share with someone I know or don’t know that well, or getting to know.

V:Would you say that social media has impacted people’s confidence over the years?

D: Yes and no, I would say it hurts people’s confidence a lot if you are out there posting a lot of things or you're sharing your life essentially with a lot of people there’s going to be people that tell you “oh my God, you’re awesome” and then your confidence goes up or they’re gonna say “oh my god you suck” and then your confidence goes down so I think it depends on how you look at it or how you take people’s criticism or compliments

V: Would you say that you felt confident when I took your picture?

D: I would say...like yes and no, you know...I am confident if anyone wants to take my picture, am I confident that they all look good? No hahaha. But I am confident enough to take pictures.
