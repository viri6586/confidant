Arielle

Confidence Chats with Random Strangers

V: Do you have social media?

A: Yes?

V: How would you say your confidence has been impacted or affected by social media if at all?

A: Hmm, That’s a hard question, hahaha. I would say...wow I’ve had social media for so long, it’s been different over the years. I would say Instagram doesn’t really help my confidence, I’m generally a really confident person, but I wouldn’t say it’s because of my instagram. I definitely don’t feel very confident with posting pictures of my own face on instagram, because I don’t want to seem like that type of person who wants to do that. My instagram is a lot less and it’s like photos of my dog.

V: What is some piece of advice, or helpful tips you have to share with people who struggle with confidence?

A: This is a hard one because confidence is a really important thing to me, I’m one of those believers that I think humility is really overrated, I think we’ve been taught it as like our parents who were mostly religious, especially being a woman, it’s like “oh you need to have a little bit of humility, you don’t wanna put yourself out there too much” but I’m a big space taker. And I think the world would be better if everyone was a little bit confident than a little bit too humble. Right, better to be a little too confident than a little too humble. I just think that having confidence in who you are, that’s what draws other people in, and that’s when you’re gonna be surrounded by awesome people and that’s when everyone else is gonna feel more confident. So by myself being personally confident or you being personally confident, it’s gonna make everyone else feel confident too. Makes for a better world.
